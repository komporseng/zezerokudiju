Meet Vivek Ramaswamy Parents

Ramaswamy earned his A.B. in biology from Harvard College in 2007, summa cum laude, and Phi Beta Kappa. His senior thesis focuses on the ethical difficulties raised by the production of human-animal chimeras.

What Does Vivek Ramaswamy Do for His Living?

Ramaswamy is currently the co-founder and Executive Chairman of Strive Asset Management, an asset management firm based in Ohio that has received funding from investors such as Peter Thiel and J. D. Vance.

Strive was formed to offer an alternative to more prominent fund managers like BlackRock, State Street, and Vanguard, whom Ramaswamy has criticized for engaging in ESG activities and combining business and politics to the detriment of shareholders.

Strive’s total assets under management surpassed $500 million on November 11, 2022, three months after the launch of its first fund.

In January 2023, Strive launched a proxy consulting service to compete with established businesses such as Glass Lewis and Institutional Shareholder Services. Ramaswamy is described as the “leading anti-ESG fighter” by Axios and Bloomberg.
