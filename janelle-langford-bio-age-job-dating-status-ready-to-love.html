Janelle Langford On Ready To Love

Janelle Langford is one of the singles appearing on Ready To Love.

In the show, Janelle bonded with Herbert Smith in the first episode of the show. Janelle described him as “looking all good, very nice, great energy” and she was here for all of it. The couple were flirting and touching one another so there definitely was a spark there between the two.

Herbert tells Janelle that he wasn’t married before and he wants kids (how many lord says). Janelle thought their interaction “wasn’t forced” and “it felt good.”

She recently took to her IG to share her experience of being on the show via a lengthy post.

“When I started the application process (September) of the Ready to Love journey, I was just coming out a tumultuous relationship. One that I walked away from still believing in love but understanding what love requires. And more importantly, what love isn’t,” she started the post. “I was a huge fan of the show so when the opportunity presented itself and my moving to Dallas finally showed purpose, I jumped on it without hesitation.”

She added that it wasn’t an enjoyable experience for her. She added, “I have a new found respect for reality TV stars. As an audience, you have absolutely no clue what you are really watching, yet form opinions without context…. I hate that for you.”

Janelle Langford Family

Janelle Langford is the daughter of James Buckins Jr. and Linda Henry Buckins. Her father passed away on 23 August 2022 in North Richland Hills, Texas.

James was born to James Davis Buckins, Sr., and Suretha Ross Buckins on 23 August 1942 in Dayton, Ohio. James was a proficient trumpet player and traveled the world extensively as a professional musician. He worked over 40 years in the aerospace industry as an engineer and auditor. He retired from Lockheed Martin in 2011.

Linda attended Columbia Union College and The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. She is a former Family Nurse Practitioner at Children’s Medical Center Dallas and Certified Nurse Practitioner at Parkland Health & Hospital System.

Janelle has other two sisters April Buckins, Jasmine (Rodney) Crimes, Nia Langford, Reola Buckins, and Renee Buckins.
