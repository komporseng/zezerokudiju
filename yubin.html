Yubin is a hardworking South Korean singer-songwriter, rapper, actress, and motivational speaker. Yubin is also famous as a former member of the girl group Wonder Girls and the founder of “Mr. Entertainment”.

Early Life

Yubin was born on October 4, 1988, and she is currently 34 years old. Yubin’s hometown is Gwangju, South Korea, and her zodiac sign is Libra. Her full name is Kim Yu-bin, written in Korean as 김유빈 and in Hanja as 金婑斌.

Education

Talking about her educational journey, Yubin attended Anyang High School for a while. Following this, Yubin moved to San Jose, California. There, Yubin graduated from Leland High School. Later on, she returned to South Korea and attended Myongji University majoring in Film and Musical.

Career and Professional Life

She is a singer-songwriter, rapper, as well as an actress. She gained international fame as a member of the South Korean girl group “Wonder Girls”. The original members of this girl group are Yeeun, Sunye, Sohee, Sunmi, and Hyuna. However, Hyuna departed from the group in July 2007, she joined the group on September 7, before they released their debut studio album called The Wonder Years the same year.

Before this, she was preparing to debut as a part of the girl group ‘Five Girls’. The other possible members of this group were G.NA, SECRET‘s Hyosung, former After School‘s UEE, and UNI.T‘s Jiwon. More prominently, she is famous as the main rapper of “Wonder Girls” and also occasionally acts as an additional vocalist.

Besides her own and the group’s music, she has also starred in numerous music videos of several artists. Some of them are 2 PM’s 10 Out of 10 and Shinhwa’s Once in a Life Time. The singer has also featured in many artists’ singles like Shinhwa’s Andy Lee, Lee Min-woo, Kim Bum-soo, and Chun G among others.

She is also a songwriter and she proved her talent initially through Kim Bum-soo’s song Do You Know That?. Following this, she has written her lyrics for many songs like 2011’s Girls Girls, Me, In, and Sweet Dreams from Wonder World; and Hey Boy from Wonder Party.

Journey as a Solo Artist

He made her official debut as an actress in the year 2013 with the TV drama The Virus. In this drama series, she played the role of the character Lee Joo Yeong. Her character is a genius hacker and an IT specialist. There was confirmation news on August 17, 2015, that she would join Unpretty Rapstar Season 2.

As a result of appearing on the reality singing show, he received wider recognition. The singer’s makeup and hairstyle also became popular in her native country. Following the disbandment of “Wonder Girls” on January 26, 2017, he was one of two members who stayed with JYP Entertainment. She stayed back as a solo artist and subsequently renewed her contract.

As a solo artist, she made her debut with the album City Woman on June 5, 2018. The retro lead single of this album is Lady. The singer then appeared on Stage K as a judge in April 2019. It is a dance competition TV show.

Furthermore, he has also appeared in King of Mask Singer as a contestant named “Snow Flower” in 2021. Before this, the singer was also a host for Things These Days alongside fellow singers Jun Hyo-Seong and Berry Good’s JoHyun.

As an actress, she has also made cameo appearances in VIP, Chocolate, and Here He Comes. She left JYP Entertainment on 25 January 2020 and went on to found her music label RRR Entertainment in February 2020. She released her single Yaya (Me Time) in May 2020, her first project under the new music label. She has also partnered with CLEF Entertainment on their project “Traveller” in December 2020. They released the special single Wave.
