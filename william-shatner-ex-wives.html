Meet Elizabeth, Nerine, Marcy and Gloria

August 2024 · 2 minute read

William Shatner is a singer, songwriter, and comedian from Canada. Shatner has a net worth of $100 million.

Shatner began his career as a stage actor before moving to television, and for a period he was only hired in minor roles in obscure films.

On March 22, 1931, in Notre-Dame-De-Grace, Montreal, Quebec, Canada, Shatner was born to Jewish parents Joseph and Anne Shatner.

He went to West Hill High School before transferring to Baron Byng High School. He first gained prominence as a member of the Montreal Children’s Theatre when he was eight years old.

Shatner earned his bachelor’s degree in 1952. Shatner was also a regular on Canadian Broadcasting Corporation radio shows.

In the realm of entertainment, Shatner is a household name.

William Shatner ex-wives: Meet Elizabeth, Nerine, Marcy and Gloria

Shatner had a charming spark about him that got women curious about persona. He never took advantage of that but married the ones he felt amazing connections with.

Who is Gloria Rand?

On August 12, 1956, he married actress Gloria Rand. She was Shatner’s first wife. They have three daughters. After 13 years, the marriage terminated in divorce. 

Who is Marcy Lafferty?

He married Marcy Lafferty in 1973, but the marriage dissolved 23 years later in 1996. 

From this marriage, he had no children.

Who is Nerine Kidd?

He married actress Nerine Kidd in 1997, but two years later, Kidd drowned in a  swimming pool as a consequence of the effects of alcohol and Valium. she was his 3rd wife.

Who is Elizabeth Anderson Martin?

Shatner got married for the fourth time to Elizabeth Anderson Martin in 2001. They divorced in 2020.

Who are William Shatner’s children?

Shatner had 3 daughters. Leslie Shatner, the oldest daughter is 63 years old. She has two children of her own, and has her an occupational therapy business. She had a part in Star Trek: The Original Series

Lisabeth Shatner, Shatner’s middle daughter, aspires to be an actress. She spends a lot of her time in West Hollywood.

Melanie Shatner, the youngest daughter, is also an actress. She is presently married to Joel Gretsch and lives in Los Angeles, California.
