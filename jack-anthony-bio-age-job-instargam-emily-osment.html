Meet Jack Anthony, Emily Osment Boyfriend-Turned-To Be Husband

Jack Anthony is Emily Osment’s fiancee who asked the actress to marry him at Yosemite National Park in California in June 2023. Based on the pics Emily shared on IG, he seems to have proposed to her not only with a two-stone ring but also with two buttons featuring pics of them from their childhood days along with the words “Will you marry me?” and “Yes!” written across the front.

Her post was captioned, “This magical, beautiful, kaleidoscope of a person asked me to marry him this weekend. I did not know life could be this sweet or I could ever be this deliriously happy.” The actress then went on to talk about how proud she was of “the life we have built together and the people we have become over the last few years.”

“This love is so big and so uniquely ours and I know it can do anything. I am so honored to stand next to you every day. I love you, Jack,” she added.

Obviously, congratulation was in order for the pair after Emily broke the big news.  “Congrats Emily!!” Daryl Sabara, the bride-to-be’s Spy Kids co-star wrote. Likewise, her Young Sheldon co-star Iain Armitage added, “Congratulations!”

Prior to the engagement, Jack and Emily kept much of their relationship private. The last we saw them was in June 2022 when they had a “big time cheesin in wine country” in Paso Robles, California. Also, a few months before that, the duo graced a friend’s wedding.

Jack Anthony Age

Jack Anthony was 30 years of age in 2023.

Jack Anthony Job

Jack Anthony worked as a partnership strategy expert and product leader at Linktree in 2023.

Before that, Jack he was the Head of Platform Partnerships at Spring for almost 2 years, and a partner manager at Treespring for over 4 years.

At Treespring, Jack served as team lead for top-tier talent acquisition and management working with brands such as the NFL and NBA, artists like Beyonce and Santana, and content creators like Phil DeFranco and Aphmau. Also, he managed business development and partner relations for global platform and agency partnerships such as YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, WME, and CAA.

Furthermore, Jack has been an Analyst at Media Predict and Account Executive at HouseCall Pro, each for six months.

Besides this, Jack’s a musician/ DJ. Find his works on Soundcloud @chronicles_of_farnia.

As for his education, Jack attended the University of Virginia for his Bachelor of Arts in Cognitive Science and Behavioral Economics from 2011 to 2015. There, he also was a founding member of the Society for Awakening Souls, a meditation, yoga, and metaphysical exploration group.

On the other hand, Emily is an actress who started her acting career performing in several commercials, including a radio spot with Dick Van Dyke, before making her film debut in The Secret Life of Girls in 1999. Now, she has over 60 movies and TV shows to her name including Young Sheldon (2023), A Very Merry Bridesmaid (2021), and Pretty Smart (2021).

Jack Anthony Family

As for his father, Tony Farina is from Charlottesville, Virginia. He went to Western Albemarle High and Hampden-Sydney College and has been Director of HVAC – Southern Virginia at Ferguson Enterprises for over 35 years. He is also the owner of P&L for Lyon, Conklin & Co.

Amy and Tony, 49 and 57 years of age respectively in 2023, recently celebrated their anniversary in August 2023.

Talking about his sibling, Luke Farina attended Godwin High and James Madison University. In 2023, he led a single life in  Mechanicsville, Virginia.

Sadly, Jack’s maternal grandmother Constance Clark Abell passed away in Jan 2022, at the age of 75.
